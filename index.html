DethGasStation

Transaction fees on Ethereum are too high to rely on the data and methods of centralized service providers. DethGasStation uses your web3 endpoint (e.g. via Metamask) to analyse gas prices of recent blocks. Note that this analysis will be about 10x faster when using a local node instead of relying on centralized service providers like Infura.
